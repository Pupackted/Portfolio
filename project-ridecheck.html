RideCheck

Vehicle component tracking app. Tracks vehicle components automatically and notifies users when they are due for maintenance.

Download on App Store

Overview

RideCheck is designed to help vehicle owners keep track of their maintenance schedule effortlessly. By automatically monitoring component usage, the app ensures that users are notified exactly when a part needs attention, preventing costly repairs and ensuring safety. With over 3,200 downloads and 11k+ impressions, it has proven to be a valuable tool for many.

Key Features

Automated Tracking

Automatically tracks mileage and usage to predict maintenance needs.

Smart Notifications

Get timely alerts when components are nearing their end of life.

Location Integration

Uses CoreLocation to provide context-aware tracking.

Widget Support

Check your vehicle status directly from your home screen.

Tech Stack

Swift SwiftUI CoreData MapKit CoreLocation CoreMotion SwiftData WidgetKit AppIntents UserNotifications
