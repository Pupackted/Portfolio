iPadOS App

AI Journal

Private journaling powered by an offline local LLM (MLX) for secure, on-device processing.

Overview

AI Journal offers a secure space for your thoughts, enhanced by the power of Artificial Intelligence. Unlike other AI apps, AI Journal runs entirely on your device using MLX, ensuring that your personal data never leaves your phone. It provides intelligent insights, mood tracking, and prompts without compromising privacy.

Key Features

Privacy First

All AI processing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to the cloud.

Local LLM

Powered by Apple's MLX framework for efficient on-device machine learning.

Smart Insights

Get AI-generated summaries and insights based on your journal entries.

Mood Tracking

Analyze the sentiment of your writing to track your mood over time.

Tech Stack

Swift MLX Local LLM
