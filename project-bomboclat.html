iOS App

BomBocLat

Pixel-art bomb-passing game inspired by “hot potato,” where players pass a ticking bomb under added twists that make the classic gameplay more fun and engaging.

Overview

BomBocLat brings the classic "hot potato" game to your phone with a retro pixel-art style. It's a high-energy party game where players must complete challenges or answer questions before passing the "bomb" to the next person. The twists and turns ensure that no two games are the same.

Key Features

Ticking Bomb Mechanic

Pass the device before the timer runs out!

Pixel Art Style

Charming retro graphics powered by SpriteKit.

Dynamic Challenges

Randomized challenges keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.

Immersive Audio

Sound effects and music managed by AVFoundation enhance the tension.

Tech Stack

Swift SwiftUI SpriteKit SwiftData Combine AVFoundation WebKit MVVM XCTest
